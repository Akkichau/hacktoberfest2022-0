WHY TO DO OPEN SOURCE?

Open Source Projects present golden opportunities for developers to share and, above all, learn through collaboration. ... Accordingly, Open Source ushers in higher quality, greater reliability, more flexibility, lower costs and an end to proprietary lock-in. So ultimately Open Source is good for business..
